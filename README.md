Global Study Arena 🌍⏱️
Level up your focus. A gamified, dark-mode Pomodoro timer designed to help students and developers build addictive study habits.

What is this?
Global Study Arena is a web application that turns boring study sessions into a game. Instead of just setting a timer on your phone and forgetting about it, you log into the Arena, lock in your focus time, and watch your permanent stats grow.

Features 🚀

Customizable Focus Limits: Choose from quick 10-minute sprints, 60-minute deep work sessions, or type in your own custom time limits.
Dynamic Visual Timer: A sleek, circular progress ring smoothly ticks down and shifts colors (from green, to yellow, to red) as you approach the finish line.
Gamified Profiles: Every time you complete a session, the minutes are permanently added to your account. Track your Total Focus Time, build a Daily Streak, and level up your rank!
Global Leaderboard: Compete against other users to see who has logged the most deep-work hours.
Distraction-Free UI: Built with a premium, glassmorphic dark-mode aesthetic to keep your eyes rested and your mind focused on the task at hand.

How to use it
Create a free account in the Arena.
Select your desired focus time from the Timer tab.
Hit Start Focus and get to work!
When the timer hits zero, a notification will pop up, and your Profile stats will instantly level up.
