Deep Work Arena ⚔️⏱️Level up your focus. A gamified, dark-mode focus timer designed to help professionals, developers, and creators build addictive deep-work habits.
What is this?Deep Work Arena is a web application that turns boring work sessions into a game. Instead of just setting a timer on your phone and forgetting about it, you log in, lock in your focus time, and watch your permanent stats grow. Enter the arena and climb the global leaderboard.
Features 
🚀Cloud Memory: Permanent PostgreSQL database that tracks your lifetime hours.
Global Leaderboard: Compete with users worldwide for the #1 spot.
Dynamic Gamification: Automatically rank up from Novice to Grandmaster.
Daily Streaks: Build unstoppable momentum with real daily streak tracking.
Anti-Cheat Precision Timer: Tab-switching protection ensures every minute earned is legitimate.
Focus Insights: Categorize your sessions and visualize your time (Spotify Wrapped style).
